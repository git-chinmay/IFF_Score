- Can change IFF to IUP Calculator 
- priority = (Impact + Urgency) * Frustration

Impact = 1 to 4
4 - Affecting entire org
3 - Afecting multiple departmets
2 - Affecting single dept 
1 - Affeting single person

Urgency = 1 to 4
4 - Critical
3 - High 
2 - Medium
1 - Low

Frustration = 1 to 4
4 = High Frustration
1 = Low Frustration
